Project Idea

Overview: Creating a website or smartphone application that lists the prices of products people want to buy, at the lowest price possible. A comparison between the original price of the product and the lowest price is to show the user the amount of cash that can be saved. This website is about purchasing a product at the lowest price possible and in the most convenient way. This will be a website that is accessible by anyone who wants to use it and is free.

Motivation: Products are not always listed at their lowest price in the market. Trying to find a cheap price for a product that is usually expensive can be time consuming. This project can assist in the search for cheap prices quick and easy.

Description: There will be a feature that shows the user the amount of cash being saved compared to the original price of the product. Another possible feature could also be showing the user the most convenient way to acquire these products whether it be online or travelling.
